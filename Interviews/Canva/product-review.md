# Canva Product Review Interview

## Overview

**Stage:** Product Review Interview
**Format:** 60 minutes total

---

## Interview Structure

### Part 1: Product Deep-Dive (25 mins)
Understand the problem the product was intended to solve, the surrounding context, and decisions made during involvement. Focus: approach to addressing key user and customer needs.

### Part 2: Technical Deep-Dive (25 mins)
How various engineering realities (constraints or opportunities) informed strategy and decision-making. Not expected to have written code — assessed on how technical considerations shaped product strategy and execution.

### Part 3: Question Time (10 mins)
Ask questions about the Product team or life at Canva.

---

## Preparation

**Task:** Use the provided template to showcase a product, feature, or initiative worked on over the past four years that best highlights core product skills and technical experience.
